
-- Note: The bingo_claims table is no longer necessary as this data is stored in the universal_game_logs table.

-- Example universal_game_logs fields for bingo claims:
-- player_id - The ID of the player making the claim
-- claimed_at - Timestamp when the claim was made
-- validated_at - Timestamp when the claim was validated (null if pending)
-- win_pattern - The pattern that was claimed
-- session_id - The session where the claim was made
-- game_number - The game number in the session
-- status - Status of the claim (pending, validated, rejected)
